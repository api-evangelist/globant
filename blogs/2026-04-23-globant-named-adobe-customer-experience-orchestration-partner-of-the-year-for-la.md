<span class="field field--name-title field--type-string field--label-hidden">Globant Named Adobe Customer Experience Orchestration Partner of the Year for LATAM</span>
<span class="field field--name-uid field--type-entity-reference field--label-hidden"><span lang="" about="/user/1056" typeof="schema:Person" property="schema:name" datatype="">yasmin.rahhal</span></span>
<span class="field field--name-created field--type-created field--label-hidden"><time datetime="2026-04-23T14:15:37-05:00" title="Thursday, April 23, 2026 - 14:15" class="datetime">Thu, 04/23/2026 - 14:15</time>

            <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p class="text-align-center" dir="ltr"><em>Powered by its proven Adobe Experience Cloud expertise, Globant revolutionizes digital marketing operations performance through AI.</em></p><p class="text-align-center" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">LAS VEGAS — April&nbsp;23, 2026 —&nbsp;<a href="https://www.globant.com/"><strong>Globant</strong></a> (NYSE: GLOB), a digitally native company that helps organizations thrive in a digital and AI-powered future, today announced it has been recognized as the Adobe Customer Experience Orchestration Partner of the Year. Adobe’s Customer Experience Orchestration Partner Awards honor companies that have made leading contributions to Adobe’s business and delivered significant impact on customer success.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">This prestigious award is presented to the LATAM partner that has demonstrated exceptional capabilities in accelerating AI adoption and driving business growth in global companies; it recognizes those who successfully leverage the latest innovations within Adobe Experience Cloud to unify data, content, consumer journeys, and activation for seamless, end-to-end customer experience orchestration.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><em>“At Globant, we think about our clients' challenges through the lens of the Brand Experience Operating System (BXOS), a foundation where content is creative, structured, machine-readable, and enriched with the signals that AI systems rely on to make decisions”</em>,&nbsp;<strong>said Ruben García Calvo, Managing Director of the MarTech Studio at Globant.</strong>&nbsp;<em>“This represents a fundamental shift from producing traditional content for channels to designing ecosystems that can be interpreted, activated, and optimized in real time; a new era where the organizations that stand out aren’t choosing between creativity and technology; they’re integrating both”.&nbsp;</em></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">“<em>Globant’s mastery of the Adobe Experience Cloud—and their commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in consumer journey orchestration—makes them an invaluable partner in our mission to transform digital businesses. We congratulate the entire team on this well-deserved achievement and look forward to our continued success together</em>”, said&nbsp;<strong>Alison Webster, VP Global Partner Organization at Adobe.&nbsp;</strong></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">Partners were recognized at the Partner Executive Forum during Adobe Summit 2026 in Las Vegas.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">With over a decade of co-innovation, Globant and Adobe have delivered more than 250 successful projects and executed over 300 deployments across diverse industries, earning more than 20 industry awards along the way. This partnership reached a new milestone with the expansion of Globant’s Adobe Studio powered by Omni.Pro. By blending deep technical expertise with the power of Adobe Experience Cloud, in 2025 Globant is excited to be a part of Adobe’s Global Platinum Partners.&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">To learn more about Globant’s Adobe Studio, visit:&nbsp;<a href="https://www.globant.com/studio/adobe">https://www.globant.com/studio/adobe</a>&nbsp;</p><p dir="ltr">__</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><strong>About Globant</strong></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">At Globant, we help organizations thrive in a digital and AI-powered future. Our industry-focused solutions combine technology and creativity to accelerate enterprise transformation and design experiences customers love. Through digital reinvention, our subscription-based AI Pods, and Globant Enterprise AI platform, we turn challenges into measurable business results and promised savings into real impact.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><ul><li dir="ltr">We have more than 28,700 employees and are present in over 35 countries across 5 continents, working for companies like FIFA, Google, Riot Games, and Santander, among others.</li><li dir="ltr">We were named a Worldwide Leader in Experience Design Services (2025), and previously recognized as a Worldwide Leader in AI Services (2023) by IDC MarketScape.</li><li dir="ltr">We were featured as a business case study at Harvard, MIT, and Stanford.</li><li dir="ltr">We are active members of The Green Software Foundation (GSF) and the Cybersecurity Tech Accord.</li><li dir="ltr">We are global partners of Open AI, NVIDIA, AWS and Unity bringing world-class technology together to accelerate innovation across industries.</li></ul><p>&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">Contact:&nbsp;<a href="mailto:pr@globant.com">pr@globant.com</a>&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><a href="https://www.globant.com/contact">Sign up</a> to get first dibs on press news and updates.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">For more information, visit&nbsp;<a href="https://www.globant.com/">www.globant.com</a>.</p><p>&nbsp;</p></div>
