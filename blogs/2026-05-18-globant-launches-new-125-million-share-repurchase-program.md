            <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><strong>LUXEMBOURG,&nbsp;18 May, 2026</strong> - Last Thursday, May 14th, the Board of Directors of Globant S.A. (NYSE: GLOB) approved a new share repurchase program, authorizing the allocation of up to $50 million per quarter, subject to a maximum aggregate of $125 million, for the repurchase of its common shares beginning today and open through the fourth quarter of 2027.&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">This decision reflects our shared conviction in a landscape where "services are the new software” and AI enables enterprises to buy outcomes rather than just tools.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">“We are at the beginning of the most consequential transformation the technology services industry has ever seen. The most influential voices in tech are investing beyond models into delivery and deployment — and the demand for cost-effective AI solutions has never been more urgent. Our AI Pods model is built precisely for this moment. This new repurchase program reflects our confidence in that direction and our commitment to delivering long-term value to shareholders,” said&nbsp;<strong>Martín Migoya, Chairman and CEO of Globant.</strong></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">"This share repurchase program is supported by our free cash flow generation, and it’s a key component of our disciplined capital allocation strategy. As we engage in this program, we will continue investing in strategic growth initiatives," added&nbsp;<strong>Juan Urthiague, Globant's CFO.</strong></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">The timing and price of repurchases as well as the actual number of shares repurchased under the program will be at the discretion of Globant and will depend on a variety of factors, including business and market conditions, the share price, regulatory requirements and limitations, corporate liquidity requirements and priorities, legal requirements and restrictions in the agreements governing our indebtedness, alternative investment opportunities, acquisition opportunities and other factors. Globant is not obligated to repurchase any specific amount of shares. The share repurchase program may be suspended or discontinued at any time or periodically without prior notice.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><strong>About Globant</strong></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">At Globant, we help organizations thrive in a digital and AI-powered future. Our industry-focused solutions combine technology and creativity to accelerate enterprise transformation and design experiences customers love. Through digital reinvention, our subscription-based AI Pods, and Globant Enterprise AI platform, we turn challenges into measurable business results and promised savings into real impact.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><ul><li dir="ltr"><p class="text-align-justify">We have more than 28,500 employees and are present in over 35 countries across 5 continents, working for companies like FIFA, Google, Riot Games, and Santander, among others.</p></li><li dir="ltr"><p class="text-align-justify">We were named a Worldwide Leader in Experience Design Services (2025), and previously recognized as a Worldwide Leader in AI Services (2023) by IDC MarketScape.</p></li><li dir="ltr"><p class="text-align-justify">We were featured as a business case study at Harvard, MIT, and Stanford.</p></li><li dir="ltr"><p class="text-align-justify">We are active members of The Green Software Foundation (GSF) and the Cybersecurity Tech Accord.</p></li><li dir="ltr"><p class="text-align-justify">We are global partners of Open AI, NVIDIA, AWS and Unity bringing world-class technology together to accelerate innovation across industries.</p><p class="text-align-justify">&nbsp;</p></li></ul><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">Contact:&nbsp;<a href="mailto:pr@globant.com">pr@globant.com</a>&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><a href="https://www.globant.com/contact">Sign up</a> to get first dibs on press news and updates.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">For more information, visit&nbsp;<a href="https://www.globant.com/">www.globant.com</a>.</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">&nbsp;</p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr"><strong>Forward Looking Statements&nbsp;</strong></p><p class="text-align-justify" dir="ltr">This press release contains "forward-looking statements" within the meaning of the Private Securities Litigation Reform Act of 1995.
